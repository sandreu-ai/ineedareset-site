Questions

Is this just junk removal?

No. Removal can be part of the reset, but the main goal is making the space functional while protecting the things you want to keep.

Do I need to clean before you see it?

No. Send the real version. That helps us quote the project accurately and recommend the right plan.

Can I send photos?

Yes. Submit the form first, then reply to our text with one photo facing the garage door, one photo facing the back wall, one photo from a corner near the garage door, rough garage dimensions if you know them, and your main goal for the space.

Are the Garage Reset Plan fees credited toward the project?

Yes. If you book a Reset project within 30 days, the full $99 virtual plan fee or $199 in-person plan fee is credited toward that project.

Is the AI mockup included?

Yes. Both Garage Reset Plans include a free AI-assisted concept mockup to help you picture possible storage, layout, and function improvements before work begins.
